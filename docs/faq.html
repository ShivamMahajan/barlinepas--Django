FAQ

What is BarLinePass?

BLP, or BarLinePass provides bar goers the ability to purchase tickets to skip the line at their favorite venues. No more planning around lines or wondering if you’ll get in. With a click of a few buttons and a little cash up front, you guarantee yourself a great night out!

Where can I use BarLinePass?

Right now BarLinePass is in New York City and Washington, DC. We are actively obtaining new partners, but if you would like to give us a suggestion request your Click Here.

Why should I use BarLinePass?

Convenience – No more wasting time in line. BarLinePass provides you with the convenience of walking up to the venue of your choice and skipping the line. Spend less time outside waiting and more time inside with your friends.

How far in advance can I book a BarLinePass?

There is no time frame to book out. Check your venues calendar and make sure they are offering a BLP the day you are looking to go.

What's included with my BarLinePass

A ticket that allows you to skip the line of the bar you bought a pass for.

I bought a BarLinePass, do I still have to pay cover?

Depends on the bar. They will specify this information on their venue page.

How many people can I bring in with my pass? How many times can I use it?

One person per QR code. Each QR code has its own unique ID.

What time should I arrive at the venue?

Your BLP is valid for the day you purchase until the venue closes. Go at the time of longest wait. It feels better that way, right?

What is the Early Arrival option?

If you see the option for Early Arrival, it means that you will get a discount if you arrive before a certain time. Not all venues offer early option.

Can I transfer my ticket?

All ticket sales are final – no changes on date or location.

Can I cancel my order?

All ticket sales are final

Once I buy a BarLinePass, when will my card be charged?

Time of purchase

Will I have to show my ID if I book through BarLinePass?

Yes, you will be required to show ID to verify your age. You must be 21 to purchase a BarLinePass!

We decided we aren’t going out after all, can we cancel?

Sorry! All ticket sales are final.

Getting Into the Venue

What type of ID should I bring to the venue?

  • Valid, unexpired, US driver's license (paper ID is not acceptable)
  • Out-of-state licenses often require a second form of ID, such as a credit card.
  • Official passport
  • U.S. Military ID

Can the door staff refuse entry if I booked through BarLinePass?

Yes, entry is entirely the discretion of the door staff.

What are some reasons that I could be denied entry to a venue?

A venue can deny anybody entry from their private building, but some major reasons are:

  • Arriving overly-intoxicated
  • Being aggressive or rowdy
  • Breaking dress code
  • Being underage
  • Invalid forms of ID
  • Failing to comply with other establishment policies (which vary from venue to venue.)

My ID is expired, can I still get into a club?

No. Expired IDs are not valid as a proof of age.

I have a temporary paper ID, what should I do?

Paper IDs are not honored as proper proof of age. Events and Tickets

I bought a ticket, where do I find it?

  • Email: We sent your pass and QR code to your email
  • If you can not find it in your email, login on top and click manage account it will show you what email address you send it to. If you logged in with facebook, relogin with facebook and hit manage account.
  • If you can still not find it, login in, click manage account then booking and payment history. If you see the pass you bought, email support@barlinepass.com

Do I need to print anything to bring to the venue?

You can print out your QR code, or pull it up on your phone when you get to the bar.

Is my ticket transferrable?

No, but you can buy a ticket for a friend and send it to them. Each BLP has its own uniqe ID so you can pay multiple passes at once and send them to friends. Once the QR code is scanned at the door, that specific QR will not work again.

I didn’t receive my ticket, what do I do?

First, check your spam folder in case it ended up there. Otherwise, Login to your account, click manage account and look at the email you have. If you can still not find it, email supprot@barlinepass.com

I bought a ticket for a friend, does it matter that it has my name on it?

The name on the tickets is not an issue. As long as you send them their unique QR code, they will be admitted to the event.

Can I cancel my BarLinePass ticket?

Tickets are non-refundable, and all sales are final.
